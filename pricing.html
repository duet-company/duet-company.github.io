Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing and plans.

Is the Starter plan really free?
Yes! The Starter plan is free forever with no time limit. You get 1GB of storage and 10,000 queries per month. Perfect for personal projects and testing.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, you'll receive credit toward future billing.
What happens if I exceed my query limit?
We'll send you a notification when you reach 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, we won't cut off your service—you can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional queries à la carte.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid plan, we'll give you a full refund—no questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise customers, we can accept bank transfers and invoices.
