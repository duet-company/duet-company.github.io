🌍 Policy & Ethics

Multiple US States Advance AI Legislation

March 12, 2026 • State Governments

Several U.S. states made significant progress on AI-related bills, including Washington, Mississippi, and New York advancing various AI regulations.

  • Washington: HB 1170 (AI disclosure), HB 2225 (chatbot safety with self-harm protocols), and SB 5395 (AI in health insurance) all received final approval
  • Mississippi: HB 1723 defining "artificial intelligence" approved and sent to governor
  • New York: S 933 (chief AI officer position) and S 934 (generative AI disclosure notices) advanced to third reading
  • Pending Bills: California, Massachusetts, and Minnesota have bills on workplace surveillance, deepfakes, and algorithmic pricing

State-level AI legislation is accelerating, creating a patchwork of regulations that businesses must navigate while operating across different jurisdictions.
