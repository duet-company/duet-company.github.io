    <h3>🧠 Microsoft Launches Phi-4-Reasoning-Vision-15B</h3>
    <p>Microsoft released Phi-4-reasoning-vision-15B, a compact <strong>15-billion-parameter multimodal AI model</strong> that matches or exceeds performance of systems many times its size. The model processes both images and text, can reason through complex math and science problems, interpret charts and documents, navigate GUIs, and handle visual tasks.</p>
    <p><strong>Key Innovation:</strong> Uses a "mixed reasoning and non-reasoning" approach — it invokes structured reasoning for domains like math and science, but defaults to fast direct responses for perception-focused tasks where thinking is unnecessary.</p>
    <p><strong>Training Efficiency:</strong> Trained on ~200B tokens (one-fifth the data of competitors) through meticulous data curation and manual QA. Available open-weight on Microsoft Foundry, HuggingFace, and GitHub.</p>
