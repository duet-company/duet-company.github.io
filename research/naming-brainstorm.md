## Criteria for Final Decision

### Must-Have:
- ✅ Unique (not "AI [something] Labs")
- ✅ Memorable (easy to recall)
- ✅ Available domain likely
- ✅ No negative connotations
- ✅ Works internationally (English-friendly)

### Nice-to-Have:
- ✅ Alliteration or wordplay
- ✅ Evokes product benefit
- ✅ Short (2-3 words max)
- ✅ Trademark-friendly

### Must-Avoid:
- ❌ Too generic ("DataFlow", "QueryAI")
- ❌ Too descriptive ("ClickHouse Analytics Platform")
- ❌ Hard to spell/pronounce
- ❌ Existing brand conflicts
